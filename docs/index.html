People don't invest for returns.
They invest to arrive somewhere.

Retirement. A house. A child's education. An emergency fund. Financial independence. A normal investor never says "maximize my Sharpe ratio." They say "don't fail my goal."

Goal-Based Wealth Management with Reinforcement Learning

Applies RL to goal-based wealth management — retirement plans, target-date funds, periodic contributions, withdrawals, and rebalancing. Proposes G-Learner: a probabilistic extension of Q-learning built for the noisy reality of financial data.

06 — The extension

A strategy shouldn't drive the same way
in every market weather.

Regime probabilities enter the state. The policy stops being stationary.

Bull
growth · risk-on
P̂ 0.42
Agent leans into equities. Adds risk because the trend rewards it.
Bear
decline · defensive
P̂ 0.18
Agent hedges. Prioritizes capital preservation over chasing return.
High-vol
unstable · risk-off
P̂ 0.21
Agent reduces turnover. Avoids decisions amplified by noise.
Stable
normal · balanced
P̂ 0.19
Agent rebalances toward the long-term glide path.
07 · Why not a trading bot?Defensible > flashy
Regime-Aware GBWM07 / 14

Trading bots look exciting.
They are weak projects.

Trading bot

"Make more money."

Drives as fast as possible.
  • Easy to overfit history
  • "Maximize return" is a weak objective
  • Lucky backtests aren't proof
  • Hard to defend academically
Goal-based RL

"Reach the goal safely."

Recalculates the route.
  • Clear, falsifiable objective
  • Honest evaluation: probability of success
  • Real-life relevance
  • Defendable claim, not a backtest

Easier to demo, plenty of templates — but feels like calling a framework, not building research.
Less original
A · 04
Goal-Based Wealth RL
Realistic, explainable, course-aligned. Strong base paper plus a clear extension. Ambitious — not unrealistic.
Chosen

10 · EvaluationMetrics that match the goal

Higher return is not the
question. Reaching the goal is.

Metric · 01

Goal attainment

How often does the strategy actually reach the target?

Metric · 02

Shortfall risk

If we fail, how bad is the gap below the target?

Metric · 03

Terminal wealth

The full distribution of outcomes — not a single number.

Metric · 04

Max drawdown

Painful losses on the path, not just the endpoint.

Metric · 05

Turnover

Trading creates costs. The agent should rebalance, not churn.

Metric · 06

Regime behavior

Does the policy actually adapt when the regime flips?
